## Question 2
**How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P?**  
B = MAP → Behavior = Motivation × Ability × Prompt  

- M (Motivation): Desire to do the habit  
- A (Ability): How easy the habit is  
- P (Prompt): Trigger to perform the habit  

To make habits easier, reduce reliance on motivation, make the habit simple, and attach it to a clear trigger.

## Question 3
**Why it is important to "Shine" or Celebrate after each successful completion of habit?**  
Celebration creates a positive emotion, which helps the brain remember and repeat the behavior. Emotions drive habits, so celebrating reinforces the habit quickly.
