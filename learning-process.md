# Learning Process

## How to Learn Faster with the Feynman Technique
1. What is the Feynman Technique?
The Feynman Technique is a method of learning where you deeply understand a topic by explaining it in very simple language as if teaching it to someone else.

## Learning How to Learn TED talk by Barbara Oakley
2. In this video, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?
The most interesting idea for me was Barbara Oakley’s explanation of the two different ways the brain learns — focused mode and diffused mode. I found it surprising that when we stop concentrating and relax, the brain can still continue processing information in the background and help us solve problems more creatively.
3. What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
* Focused (Active) Mode of Thinking:
This mode happens when we pay close attention to a task and think deeply about it. It is used while solving problems, studying difficult concepts, or concentrating on coding and calculations.
* Diffused Mode of Thinking:
This mode happens when the mind is relaxed and not actively focusing on the problem. Activities like walking, sleeping, showering, or taking breaks help the brain make broader connections and improve understanding.

## Learn Anything in 20 hours
4. According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? Only mention the points.
* Deconstruct the skill
* Learn enough to self-correct
* Remove practice barriers
* Practice for at least 20 hours
5. What are some of the actions you can take going forward to improve your learning process?
* Focus completely while studying or practicing.
* Keep my phone on silent and reduce distractions.
* Practice deep work and concentrated learning.
* Break difficult topics into smaller parts.
* Understand concepts instead of memorizing them.
* Explain concepts in my own words to improve understanding.
* Practice coding and implementation regularly.
* Take notes during learning and technical discussions.
* Track my time and follow deadlines properly.
* Ask for help and communicate obstacles clearly.
