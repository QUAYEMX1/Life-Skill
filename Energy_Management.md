# Energy Management

### 1. What are the activities you do that make you relax - Calm quadrant?

- Listening to music
- Taking a walk
- Watching movies or videos
- Spending time with friends and family
- Playing games or sports
- Meditation and deep breathing
- Sleeping or taking short naps
- Using social media for a short time
- Journaling and self-reflection

---


### 2. When do you find getting into the Stress quadrant?

- During exams or deadlines
- While learning difficult new concepts
- When handling multiple tasks together
- Lack of sleep or rest
- Family or personal pressure
- Fear of failure or making mistakes
- Public speaking or interviews

---


### 3. How do you understand if you are in the Excitement quadrant?

- Feeling motivated and energetic
- Enjoying the work or activity
- Feeling curious and engaged
- Having a positive mindset
- Feeling confident and focused
- Losing track of time while doing something enjoyable

# Sleep is your Superpower

### 4. Paraphrase the video in brief-

- Sleep improves memory and learning
- Proper sleep boosts immunity
- Sleep improves focus and productivity
- Lack of sleep increases stress and anxiety
- Good sleep supports emotional balance
- Sleep helps the brain and body recover
- Poor sleep can affect physical health
- Deep sleep is important for overall well-being

---

### 5. What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?

- Maintain a fixed sleep schedule
- Avoid mobile phones before sleeping
- Reduce caffeine intake at night
- Keep the room dark and quiet
- Exercise regularly
- Avoid heavy meals before bed
- Practice meditation or relaxation
- Sleep for at least 7–8 hours daily
