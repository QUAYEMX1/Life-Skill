# Grit and Growth Mindset

## 1. Grit
1. Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines.  
Grit is the ability to stay committed and work hard toward long-term goals despite challenges. Success comes more from consistent effort and persistence than natural talent.

---

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset 
2. Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines.  
A growth mindset is the belief that skills and intelligence can be developed through effort, learning, and persistence, rather than being fixed.

---

## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control
3. What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?   
Internal locus of control means believing that your outcomes are determined by your own actions and decisions.  
* Taking responsibility for your actions increases motivation and helps you improve continuously.

---

## 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset
4. What are the key points mentioned by the speaker to build a growth mindset (explanation not needed)?  
- Believe abilities can improve with effort  
- Embrace challenges  
- Learn from criticism  
- Persist despite failures  
- Focus on learning instead of results  
- Replace negative thoughts with positive ones  
- Take consistent action  

---
