# Prevention of Sexual Harassment

 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?  

- Unwanted physical contact or advances  
- Sexual comments, jokes, or inappropriate remarks  
- Sending offensive messages, images, or videos  
- Repeated unwanted communication or stalking  
- Demanding sexual favours  
- Bullying, intimidation, or creating an uncomfortable environment  
- Making inappropriate gestures or staring  
- Any behaviour that makes a person feel unsafe, uncomfortable, or disrespected  

2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?  

- Stay calm and clearly communicate that the behaviour is unacceptable  
- Report the incident to the appropriate authority or PoSH committee  
- Support the affected person if witnessing such behaviour  
- Keep records or evidence of repeated incidents if possible  
- Avoid encouraging or ignoring inappropriate behaviour  
- Follow workplace policies and cooperate during investigation.
