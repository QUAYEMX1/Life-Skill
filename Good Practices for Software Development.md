# Good Practices for Software Development

1. Which point(s) were new to you?  
- The importance of over-communicating with team members.  
- Using group channels instead of private messages for better visibility.  
- Tracking productivity and focus using apps.  
- Asking questions properly with clear explanations and screenshots.  
- Joining meetings early to build better communication with teammates.  

2. Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas to make progress in that area?  
I need to improve my communication and focus during work. Sometimes I hesitate to ask questions clearly or get distracted while working.  
- Write down problems clearly before asking for help.  
- Practice deep work by avoiding distractions and social media during work hours.  
- Keep my phone on silent while studying or coding.  
- Communicate updates regularly with teammates.  
- Spend more time understanding requirements before starting implementation.  
- Track daily progress and maintain consistency.
